Ranking Movie Musicals

So, I am a huge theatre kid I love musicals maybe it is because I always want to break out in song. So, I made a YouTube video about this you can check it out by using the link down below, but I decided to write down in more detail on each musical but if you want a short summary about what I think you know where to click. This is going to be almost a critic and feedback on each movie I am also going to include my thoughts on my honorable mentions as I only said their names on my video. Personally, I find it easier to write down my opinion or in this case type it down because I am still getting used to talking on a screen so freely. I left a bunch of musicals out of my video because even though some might say they are movie musicals I do not these are movies such as Bohemian Rhapsody which i consider more of a biography which had to add music numbers because the biography is about a musician. So, most of these movies are mainly musicals that were strictly in the musical genre of things.

For my top ten favourite musicals the first is Teen Beach Movie which if you do not know is a Disney musical product and no, it is not High School Musical in my a opinion it is even better maybe because I grew up more on Teen Beach Movie. The thing I feel about High School Musical is that because it was the O.G Disney teen musical so it wil always be in my heart but there is something about teen beach movie that I liked it was just a fresh air from all the musicals that tried to follow them like, no hate Camp Rock. But like most Disney original movies it felt like a bunch of plots put together like at first it waas good but when they did the whole mad scientist thing and the weather machine it really ruined the story as it just came out of nowhere and just frankly was not needed. Even though the acting and plot are cheesy this movie is nostalgic, the movie is about 2 teens who loves surfing but get warped into their favorite movie where bikers and surfers do not get along. Although Mack gets annoying from time to time. Honestly, it is not a horrible movie
